Our Philosophy

Discipline is not a motivation problem. It is a design problem.

When daily life requires constant decision-making, consistency breaks down. The Habit Method focuses on reducing decisions, lowering friction, and designing systems that make disciplined action easier to repeat—day after day.

Reduce Decisions

Every decision you make depletes mental energy. Design systems that eliminate the daily negotiation.

Lower Friction

Make the right actions easy and the wrong actions hard. Environment shapes behavior.

Systems Over Goals

Goals are destinations. Systems are the path. Focus on what you do daily, not someday.
